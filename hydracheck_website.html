Smart. Affordable. Real-Time Water Quality Monitoring.

HydraCheck Systems offers accessible IoT-powered water testing for Filipino households, farms, schools, and industries. Our Bluetooth-enabled sensor and mobile app provide instant, understandable water quality insights anytime, anywhere.

About Us

HydraCheck was founded with a mission to democratize water safety testing in the Philippines. With millions relying on costly laboratories and unreliable chemical kits, we aim to make testing accurate, accessible, affordable, and actionable through IoT sensors and interpreted mobile insights tailored to local needs.

Our Product

  • Smart Sensor: ESP32-based pH sensor with real-time monitoring.
  • Mobile App: Shows instant interpreted readings (Safe, Acidic, Basic), trend logs, and recommended actions.
  • Offline Function: Works seamlessly in rural and low-signal regions.
  • Coming Soon: Temperature & TDS sensors, cloud sync, reports, water purification suggestions.

Market Opportunity

The Philippines' water testing market is rapidly expanding with rising health awareness and digital adoption. With 27.5 million households and a PHP 200B aquaculture industry, HydraCheck targets tech-ready homes and local farms through scalable, affordable production.

How It Works

  1. Power and connect the HydraCheck sensor via Bluetooth.
  2. Dunk the probe into the water sample.
  3. Receive instant pH readings with clear interpretations.
  4. Review history logs, graphs, and recommended actions.

Who We Serve

  • Households: Affordable and simplified drinking water safety checks.
  • Aquaculture Farms: Data-driven pond monitoring for yield improvement.
  • Students & Schools: STEM-friendly hands-on learning kits.
